Ancient8 is developing the infrastructure for GameFi, with a focus on software and community. Ancient8 is building GameFi Identity and Launchpad products to serve as the marketing distribution channel for GameFi and Metaverse projects to onboard millions of users. As Vietnam’s largest blockchain gaming guild, Ancient8 enables everyone to build the Metaverse through blockchain software products, guild scholarship, blockchain education, institutional-grade research, and community. Ancient8’s vision is to democratize social and financial access in the Metaverse.

What makes Ancient8 different?

We believe that the foundation for Gaming Guilds is the same business model of managing scholarships and players for playing blockchain games. However, each Guild will have its own focus beyond the foundation. Some are focused on investing in and incubating gaming projects, while some are focused on building guild-as-a-service software. We think that the most important competitive advantage of a gaming guild comes from its community. Ancient8 is focused on building the community layer (Ancient8 Community Alliance) and the infrastructure layer (GameFi Identity Product and GameFi Token and NFT Launchpads) for the GameFi ecosystem. Additionally, geographically, Ancient8 is the largest gaming guild in Vietnam, which is considered to be one of the most influential crypto markets. - Vietnam ranks 1st on the 2021 Global Crypto Adoption Index and 2nd on the 2021 DeFi Adoption Index, according to Chainalysis. - Vietnam is also placed on the top spot on crypto ownership with ~40% of the population having invested in crypto, according to Finder - Vietnam has the 3rd highest MAU on MetaMask out of all countries as of August 2021, according to MetaMask
