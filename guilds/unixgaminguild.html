UNIX Gaming was established in July of 2021 and is one of the largest guilds in the space with 200,000 Discord members and 5000 scholars. UNIX consists of a core team of lifelong gamer's. They have evolved from a guild to a complete gaming ecosystem in a short space of time. The UNIX team believe in putting the community first whilst they fulfill their mission of bridging the gap between web 2 and web 3 gaming.

What makes UniX Gaming Guild different?

At a time when guilds were first exploding onto the scene in 2021, UNIX established its position early on as a guild for the true gamer, not one merely for the speculators and the ‘flippers’. Led by a team of life long gamer's, their core mission is to facilitate the sectors transition from web 2 to web 3. All of the products being built by the team have that vision in mind. UNIX has now grown into a complete gaming ecosystem, consisting of a not only a guild, but a launchpad, a game studio and marketplace. They are intent on becoming one of the leading tech and content creators in the space.
